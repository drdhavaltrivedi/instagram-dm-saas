## SEO Optimizations Implemented

### Sitemap Enhancements
**File:** `src/app/sitemap.ts`

**Changes:**
1. **Smart Priority System:**
   - Pillar posts: 0.95 priority
   - Featured posts: 0.90 priority
   - 2025 content: +0.05 priority boost
   - Regular posts: 0.80 priority

2. **Dynamic Change Frequency:**
   - < 3 days old: hourly
   - < 7 days old: daily
   - < 30 days old: weekly
   - Older: monthly

3. **Benefits:**
   - Encourages faster crawling of new content
   - Prioritizes important content
   - Signals freshness to search engines
   - Improves indexing speed

### Robots.txt Optimization
**File:** `public/robots.txt`

**Enhancements:**
1. **Crawl-delay: 0** for Googlebot and Bingbot
   - Allows fastest possible crawling
   - No rate limiting for major search engines

2. **Explicit Allow Rules:**
   - `/blog/` - All blog content
   - `/tools/` - All tools pages
   - `/ebook/` - Lead magnet pages
   - Special priority for 2025 content

3. **Strategic Disallows:**
   - API routes
   - Admin areas
   - Auth pages
   - Internal directories

4. **Benefits:**
   - Faster discovery of new content
   - Better resource allocation for crawlers
   - Improved indexing efficiency
   - Clear crawl priorities
